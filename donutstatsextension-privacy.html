DonutStats Privacy Policy

DonutStats is a browser extension that lets users look up public DonutSMP player statistics by entering a Minecraft username.

DonutStats does not collect, sell, or share personal information.

The extension may store recent searches, favorite usernames, and basic preferences locally in the user's browser so the extension can function properly.

DonutStats may request public player-stat data from supported DonutSMP stat sources when the user searches for a username. This data is used only to display results inside the extension.

DonutStats does not collect passwords, payment information, health information, personal communications, browsing history, or location data.
